About Us

The NEXT,It is an ISO Certified English Medium Senior Secondary School affiliated to CBSE. The school is situated on a 10-acre campus .

Our Aim

The aim of the institution is to impart holistic quality education to its pupils. Governed by the education policy of the THE NEXT EDUCATION , this premier educational institution has been rendering yeoman services to the field of education in the region.

We have a team of well qualified staff striving to make the school ‘Smart, Clean, Safe and Happy NEXT’. Our teachers are in different categories as shown below:

PRT : 26
TGT : 13
PGT : 06
PET : 02

We believe that students learn better under an atmosphere that is open, caring and conducive to learning and that collaboration between students, parents, teachers and management can together build communities, strengthen educational opportunities and develop strong value system.

Our Mission

To provide qualitative and competitive education to all the enrolled students irrespective of their social background or intellectual disparity, through an endless effort to better the good.

Our Vision

To make education relevant, interesting, meaningful and holistic so as to enrich every student’s potential, benefitting oneself and the society at large.
